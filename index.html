🧠 Sorting Algorithms Explained

Understand the rhythm behind each sort — where data meets choreography.

🫧 Bubble Sort

Compares adjacent elements, pushes the largest to the end slowly — like bubbles rising through logic.

Beginner-friendly • Stable Time: O(n²)

🧩 Merge Sort

Divides arrays into halves, sorts them recursively, then merges beautifully — like puzzle pieces fitting perfectly.

Efficient • Divide & Conquer Time: O(n log n)

⚡ Quick Sort

Picks a pivot, places smaller elements on the left, larger on the right — like slicing chaos with precision.

Fastest in practice • In-place Time: O(n log n)
